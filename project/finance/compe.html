Competitive Benchmarking and Financial Analysis

Jan 10, 2025
#Finance #Strategy

The Competitive Benchmarking and Financial Analysis project focuses on assessing Bharat Forge's global competitiveness using strategic frameworks and advanced benchmarking techniques.

  • Applied two strategic frameworks: Porter’s Diamond and C-APP, to evaluate Bharat Forge's global positioning in the market.
  • Developed five benchmarking tables to identify performance gaps and planned interviews with employees for actionable strategic insights.
  • Utilized the Quick Benchmarking Method (QBM) to analyze seven key metrics, comparing Bharat Forge with its competitors.
