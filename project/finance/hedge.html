Hedging Fixed-Income Portfolios Using Swaps

Jan 10, 2025
#Finance #Investment

This project focuses on optimizing fixed-income portfolios by leveraging interest rate swaps to hedge against market risks and maximize portfolio returns.

  • Utilized the Bloomberg terminal to monitor the bond market, analyze spreads, and evaluate the efficacy of 10+ swap strategies.
  • Optimized a fixed-income portfolio by filtering 100+ bonds based on yield curve analysis and other key market factors.
  • Developed customized interest rate swap strategies to hedge against duration and convexity risks in bond portfolios.
