Project Agastya Jr.

Jan 4, 2025
#Aerospace #Engineering

Project Agastya Jr. was an ambitious rocket development project led by the IIT Bombay rocket team, successfully launching a rocket to an altitude of 1 km. The project involved rigorous testing, analysis, and innovation to achieve outstanding performance and reliability in rocket design and deployment.

  • Assisted in the critical design and testing for the rocket, ensuring optimal functional reliability.
  • Performed 5+ composite layups and thoroughly analyzed the design, optimizing structural integrity for rocket launches.
  • Conducted detailed load cell testing for parachute deployment, ensuring enhanced stability and reliability during descent.
  • Played a key role in designing, fabricating, and assembling the launch guides and rails used for rocket deployment.
