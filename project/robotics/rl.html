Reinforcement Learning-Driven Stock Trading

Jan 4, 2025
#MachineLearning #Finance #AI

This project focuses on leveraging reinforcement learning to build an advanced stock trading platform capable of optimizing strategies and achieving consistent growth in financial portfolios.

  • Engineered a stock trading environment with Python and OpenAI Gym, processing over 30 stock dimensions and fees.
  • Deployed backtesting algorithms with an initial capital of $1 million, achieving consistent portfolio growth of 5-7%.
  • Developed and optimized a state space with 181 features, including MACD, RSI, and ADX, to improve agent decision-making.
  • Simulated agent performance, improving strategy accuracy by 15% through historical price data for reward calculations.
