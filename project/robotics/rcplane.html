RC Aircraft

Jan 4, 2025
#Aerospace #Teamwork

The RC Aircraft was a freshman year project built during a competition organized by the Aeromodelling Club. Our team of four constructed the entire plane by hand, starting from raw materials to a fully functional aircraft.

  • Collaborated in a team of four to build an RC plane entirely from scratch, showcasing strong teamwork and innovation.
  • Designed the aircraft framework, cutting and assembling materials manually to achieve aerodynamic stability.
  • Successfully participated in a competitive event, demonstrating the plane’s operational efficiency and maneuverability.
