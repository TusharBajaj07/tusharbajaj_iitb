Fracture Cast Moulding using FDM

Jan 4, 2025
#Healthcare #Manufacturing #Entrepreneurship

The Fracture Cast Moulding project replaces traditional plaster of Paris casts with 3D-printed custom casts, offering enhanced comfort, stability, and recovery for patients.

  • Developed 3D-printed wrist fracture casts using FDM technology, improving patient comfort and recovery outcomes.
  • Planned integration of advanced 3D scanning software to automate and streamline the production of custom casts.
  • Collaborated with BETiC Lab to explore startup opportunities, presenting the fracture cast and receiving 50+ valuable feedback responses.
  • Conducted in-depth research on fracture healing processes to optimize the design of 3D-printed casts for improved patient outcomes.
  • Secured a funding of ₹1,00,000 INR from SANDBOX, MBF to support project development and execution.
