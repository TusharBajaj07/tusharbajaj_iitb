Pioneering Robotics Workshop for Young Innovators

June 2024
#Education #Robotics #Entrepreneurship

The Pioneering Robotics Workshop brought hands-on learning experiences to local students, inspiring innovation and practical engineering skills in robotics.

  • Organized the first-ever robotics workshop in my hometown, introducing students to the exciting world of robotics and practical learning.
  • Taught participants to build a functional hand-gesture controlled robot, using practical engineering techniques and tools.
  • Generated a revenue of ₹20,000 INR, showcasing strong demand and receiving highly positive community feedback.
  • Achieved significant publicity through a news channel with over 500k followers, expanding the workshop's reach and impact.
