Compact Quadruped Robot

Jan 4, 2025
#Robotics #Automation

The Compact Quadruped Robot is an innovative robotic dog powered by AI and designed for advanced terrain navigation. This project combines cutting-edge technology and hardware to achieve agility and precision in robotics.

  • Engineered using PyTorch, NumPy, SolidWorks, Tinkercad, Arduino, and ESP32 components.
  • Implemented ML algorithms and neural networks with Conda, OpenCV, and TensorFlow.
  • Directed hardware development, including laser cutting and PCB design, creating an innovative voice-activated robot dog.
